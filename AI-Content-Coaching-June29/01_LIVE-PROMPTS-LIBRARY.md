### P0.1 — The GGC Brand Brief (paste into Claude at the very start, pin it)
Use this as the "brain" every later prompt references. Say live: *"This is the only thing that separates a pro from someone getting garbage."*

```
You are my brand content director. For the rest of this session, every asset you create is for this client. Hold this brief in memory and apply it to everything:

BRAND: GGC — Gym Girls Club (legal: GGC ACTIVE SDN. BHD., Malaysia)
CATEGORY: Women's fitness & lifestyle brand + girls-only community
FOUNDER/FACE: Sabina Fogiel, 27 — quit a finance job (Investment Controlling at Continental), bought a one-way ticket to Malaysia, built her own fitness transformation, runs + races Hyrox, started a girls-only running club that grew to ~80 members.
CORE LINE: HOT GIRLS WORK HARD  (architecture: "HOT GIRLS" is the locked core, the second line flexes: HOT GIRLS RUN / DO HYROX / SUPPORT GIRLS)
MISSION: Help women get fitter, more confident and more supported — together, not in competition.
VOICE: Soft-feminine led, bold accent. 80% warm/encouraging/elegant, 20% cheeky/punchy. "Your most stylish, most disciplined best friend who genuinely wants you to win." Never shame, never "no excuses," never body-comparison, never male-gaze. "Hot" = strong, confident, glowing from effort — defined by her.
PALETTE: mauve/dusty rose #D295AC (primary), soft blush #F4D9E4, light pink #FBE9F0, white #FFFFFF, warm cream #E6E2D6, ink for text #3A2730. Never harsh black, never dark by default.
TYPE FEEL: elegant high-contrast serif (Didot/Playfair) for hero lines; clean wide-tracked sans for labels; bold condensed sans (Bebas/Anton) for punchy carousel covers.
IMAGERY: real women training (running, gym, Hyrox), soft pink/mauve grade, athleisure in pinks/whites/neutrals, aspirational but real, feminine, glowing. Empowering never objectifying.
FIRST PRODUCT: "Hot Girls Work Hard" shaker — clear body, soft pink/lilac lid, pink serif print. First 200 units. Scarcity angle: "first 200, then gone."
AUDIENCE: women ~20–35 in/around Kuala Lumpur, arriving from Instagram, three overlapping types: (1) beginner/restart girls, (2) running & Hyrox girls, (3) aesthetic/lifestyle girls.
PRIMARY CTA: join the club (free) first; buy second.

Confirm you've absorbed this in 2 lines, then wait for my next instruction.
```

### P9.2 — Mistakes, shown as before/after (talk track, not paste)
The 7 documented mistakes to demo as before/after:
1. No brand brief → generic slop. (Fix: P0.1)
2. No reference image → inconsistent products. (Fix: P0.2)
3. Permissions "Always Allow" → silent runaway generations/credit burn.
4. No STATUS column → duplicate generations. (Fix: P8.2)
5. Vague video prompts / multiple actions per shot → mush. (Fix: P6.4)
6. Quoting costs you didn't verify → wrong numbers on stage / to clients.
7. Generating at full volume before testing one → 100 bad videos instead of 1 test. (Fix: test 1 → approve → batch.)
